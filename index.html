    • Introduction

    My name is Avery Triplett and I am a senior in electrical engineering at Mississippi State University. This website contains the details of my dissection for my Embedded Systems Class. I chose to dissect the Hasbro Gaming Simon Micro Series. This is a smaller version of the classic game Simon. I wanted to dissect this product because I liked memory games as a kid.

    Lessons Learned

    I gained a deeper understanding of how elctronic componenets interact within a circuit. This hands-on experience helped solidify the theoretical knowledge I'v learned in class and provided insights into real-world applications.

     

    I better understand how to identify faulty componenets, trace signals through the circuit, and decipher the purpose of certain componenets. I also improved upon my documentation skills by replicating my findings using a bill of materials, circuit schematic, and flowchart.

     

    I found that researching the device's retail information and history helped me analyze the device and draw meanignful conclusions. I also understand a bit more about the device's market appeal and design.

     

    Finally, I learned how to communicate complex technical information through a website. This helped improved my ability to translate technical concepts into a format that many people from many backgrounds can understand.
